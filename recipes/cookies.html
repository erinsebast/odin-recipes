Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

I love chocolate chip cookies. They can do me no wrong

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps:

  1. Mix melted butter, brown sugar, and eggs
  2. Mix in flour and baking powder
  3. Add chocolate chips
  4. Bake at 350 for 13-16 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
