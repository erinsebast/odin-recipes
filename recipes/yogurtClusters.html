Chocolate Strawberry Yogurt Clusters

Description

This has been my fixation lately

Ingredients:

  • Strawberries
  • Yogurt
  • Chocolate

Steps:

  1. Cut up strawberries
  2. Mix it with the yogurt
  3. Freeze for an hour or so
  4. Coat it in melted chcoalte
  5. Freeze for about 15 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
